üéØ Training Best Practices

üì∏ Image Quality Guidelines

  • Resolution: Minimum 800x600 pixels for ID cards
  • Lighting: Even, natural lighting without shadows
  • Focus: Sharp, clear text with no motion blur
  • Angle: Straight-on view, minimal perspective distortion
  • Background: Clean, contrasting background

üìä Data Collection Strategy

  • Quality over Quantity: 50 high-quality samples > 200 poor samples
  • Diverse Conditions: Include various lighting, angles, and qualities
  • Field Balance: Ensure all fields (name, ID, date) are represented
  • Edge Cases: Include challenging samples gradually

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Low Accuracy: If your model accuracy is below 85%, try adding more high-quality training samples with diverse conditions.
Slow Training: Large batch sizes or poor quality images can slow training. Use the quality assessment feature to filter samples.
Field-Specific Issues: If certain fields (like Khmer names) have low accuracy, focus on collecting more samples for those specific fields.
